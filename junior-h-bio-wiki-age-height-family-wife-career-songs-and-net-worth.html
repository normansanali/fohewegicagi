Junior H Career

Junior hopped into the scene in February 2019 with the release of his first album, a nine-song demo titled “Mi vida en un Cigarro” after a few years on YouTube. The album contained songs such as “El de la Chevy,” “El Barbero,” “A los 19,” “El de la Chevy,” and his first big hit, “No he cambiado.” Nevertheless, the song would take several months to become a trend on YouTube. The success was such that Junior, then 17 years old, was found by his now manager, Jimmy Humilde, of Rancho Humilde.

However, it didn’t take long for Junior to sign a contract and start documenting with the label. He was included in the successful collection of Natanael Cano, “Corridos Tumbados,” in the songs “Ella,” “El AK,” “Disfruto lo Malo,” and “El M4.” Thanks to this work, his popularity continued to grow. Thereafter came the singles “Vivo a Mi Manera,” “El de la H,” and “En Mis Dedos un Gatillo”.
