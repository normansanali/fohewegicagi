Shop Jennifer Lopezs Favorite Fall Boots From DSW

Jennifer Lopez (A.K.A. the marvelous “Jenny From the Block”) is one of our generation’s best-dressed celebs. The singer and actress is capable of taking a staple piece, like the pinstripe dress she wore to the Ralph Lauren spring/summer 2023 fashion show, and elevating it to a T. The new Mrs. (thank you, Ben Affleck) is also a fashion rule-breaker — which we’re all about. Not only did she make a case for post-Labor Day whites, but she also shifted the style standards by wearing a sundress in the fall.

So when J.Lo talks (or, er, dresses), we listen. Most recently, the JLo Beauty founder shared her “favorite” fall boot in a recent JLO Jennifer Lopez Footwear campaign: the $150 Lakel Over-the-Knee Boot. You know we're all ears. 

Shop now: $150; dsw.com

It’s obvious as to why J.Lo is head over heels for this pair of shoes; the sleek, medium-brown over-the-knee boot includes a daring stacked heel, synthetic lug sole, and thigh-grazing shaft height. The dual lace-up and zipper closure adds function to the daring look, making it easy to get in and out of your pair.

In the brand’s latest campaign imagery, the autumn boots are paired with thick, gold rings, an oversized, tan hoodie, and a heavyweight, fur coat. Though, you can match them with casual jeans and a cozy sweater. 

Lopez isn’t the only one in love with the over-the-knee boot trend. Stars like Margot Robbie, Katie Holmes, and Amal Clooney have been spotted wearing the bold trend, so it’s safe to say the tall boot is here to stay. Who knows? Maybe they’ll rock the Lakel Over-the-Knee Boot next. 

While the cognac-colored boot is Lopez’s “favorite” pick for autumn, the JLO Jennifer Lopez Footwear seasonal collection doesn't start and end there. “I wanted my customers and fans to have a little bit of everything — and I can’t wait to see how they wear this collection!” Lopez exclusively shared with InStyle. DSW shoppers can also find newly released chocolate-colored stiletto boots, strappy booties, and funky, ankle-wrapped platforms.

Fair warning: When J.Lo calls something her “favorite,” it sells out quickly, so we doubt these styles will last long. Get your hands on the Lakel boot, plus more options below and on the DSW site, while you still can.
