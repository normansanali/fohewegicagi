How Much Did Kirstie Alley Make?

Kirstie Alley was an American actress, model, and producer who died with a net worth of $40 million.

Kirstie Alley died on December 5, 2022, at the age of 71, following a private battle with cancer.

Kirstie Alley is best known for her role as Rebecca Howe on the NBC sitcom “Cheers.” Her performance earned her an Emmy and a Golden Globe.

She was later nominated for Emmys for her roles in the sitcom “Veronica’s Closet” and the crime series “The Last Don,” and she won another for the television film “David’s Mother.”

Among Alley’s other film credits in the 90s are John Carpenter’s “Village of the Damned”; the Olsen twins comedy “It Takes Two”; Woody Allen’s “Deconstructing Harry”; the comedy “For Richer and Poorer,” costarring Tim Allen; and the mockumentary “Drop Dead Gorgeous.”

Alley did fewer films in the 2000s and 2010s, appearing only in “Back by Midnight,” “Syrup,” and “Accidental Love” over that time.
