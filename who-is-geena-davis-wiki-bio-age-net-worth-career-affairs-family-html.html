Who is Geena Davis? | Wiki, Bio, Age, Net Worth, Career, Affairs,Family

Spread the love             

Geena Davis, a multi-talented American actress, film producer, writer, former model, and former archer, hails from Wareham, Massachusetts. Her career is filled with memorable movie roles in films like “Thelma & Louise,” “A League of Their Own,” “Beetlejuice,” “The Long Kiss Goodnight,” “Stuart Little,” “The Fly,” and “The Accidental Tourist,” to name a few. Over the years, she has been honored with prestigious awards for her outstanding performances.

  • Geena Davis received the Academy Award for “Best Supporting Actress” in 1989 for her role in “The Accidental Tourist.” She also won the Golden Globe Award for Best Actress for her work in the TV series “Commander in Chief.”

Geena Davis: Personal Life and Marriages

  • Throughout her life, Geena Davis has been married four times, with all marriages ending in divorce. Her most recent marriage with Reza Jarrahy concluded in November 2017.

Geena Davis: Early Life and Education

  • Born on September 22, 1956, in Wareham, Massachusetts, Geena Davis is currently 62 years old. Her father, William F. Davis, worked as a civil engineer and church deacon, and her mother, Lucille Davis, was a school teacher’s assistant. She shared a close bond with her mother and had an older brother named Danforth Davis.
  • Geena attended Wareham High School and later pursued a degree in Drama from Boston University.

Geena Davis: Relationships and Children

  • Geena Davis has been married to Richard Emmolo, Jeff Goldblum, Renny Harlin, and Reza Jarrahy. From her marriage with Reza Jarrahy, she has three children: Kaiis Steven Jarrahy, Kian William Jarrahy, and Alizeh Keshvar Davis Jarrahy.

Interesting Facts about Geena Davis

  • Geena made her debut on the silver screen with the movie “Tootsie” in 1982.
  • In 2017, she was nominated for a Fangoria Chainsaw Award for Best TV Actress for her role in the TV series “The Exorcist,” where she portrayed the character Angela Rance based on the novel by William Peter Blatty.
  • She also played Dr. Nicole Herman in “Grey’s Anatomy.”
  • Geena started her career as a mannequin for Ann Taylor and later signed with New York’s Zoli modeling agency.
