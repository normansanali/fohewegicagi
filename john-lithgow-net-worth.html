John Lithgow Net Worth: How Much Is John Lithgow Worth?

John Lithgow Net Worth: How Much Is John Lithgow Worth?: John Lithgow, also known as John Arthur Lithgow is an American actor.

He developed a passion for acting at a younger age, has been consistent throughout his career, and has become one of the most sought-after actors.

What Is John Lithgow Net Worth?

As of October 2023, John Lithgow has an estimated net worth of about $60 million. He has accrued such fortune from his career as an actor and has achieved great success in his career.

Lithgow studied at Harvard University and the London Academy of Music and Dramatic Art before becoming known for his diverse work on the stage and screen

His film credits include; Footloose, Harry & the Hendersons, The Pelican Brief, A Civil Action, Shrek, Kinsey, Dreamgirls, Love Is Strange, Miss Sloane, Beatriz at Dinner, Late Night, and Bombshell.

Lithgow is a recipient of numerous accolades including; 6 Emmy Awards, 2 Golden Globe Awards, 3 Screen Actors Guild Awards, and 2 Tony Awards.

He received Primetime Emmy Awards for his performances as Arthur Mitchell in Dexter and as Winston Churchill in The Crown.

Lithgow received a star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame in 2001 and he was inducted into the American Theater Hall of Fame in 2005.
