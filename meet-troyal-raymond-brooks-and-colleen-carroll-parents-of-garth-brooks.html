Meet Troyal Raymond Brooks and Colleen Carroll, parents of Garth Brooks

Troyal Raymond Brooks and Colleen Carroll are celebrity parents who happen to be the parents of Country singer, Garth Brooks.

Troyal Raymond Brooks Jr. is the father of the country singer. He was a draftsman for an oil company. He was born to his parents in 1931 but sadly passed away in 2010.

Colleen Carroll on the other hand was a 1950s-era country singer of Irish ancestry who recorded on the Capitol Records label and appeared on Ozark Jubilee. She was born to her parents in 1929 but passed away in 1999.

They were both married and parents before meeting. Troyal was the father of one before meeting his wife. He had a son called Mike Brooks. Carroll on the other hand had three children before meeting her husband. She had a daughter and two sons who have been identified as Betsy Smittle, Jim Smittle and Jerry Smittle.

Together, they have two sons – Garth and his brother who has been identified as Kelly Brooks.
