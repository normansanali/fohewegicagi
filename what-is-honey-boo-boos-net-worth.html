What is Honey Boo Boo's net worth?

HONEY Boo Boo first stepped into the limelight as a five-year-old competing in beauty pageants on TLC's Toddlers & Tiaras.

After finding success on the TLC show, she then got her own spin-off reality series, Here Comes Honey Boo Boo, which has helped her earn a fortune.

What is Honey Boo Boo's net worth?

Over the years, Alana "Honey Boo Boo" Thompson has been seen on several TLC shows, including Toddlers & Tiaras, Here Comes Honey Boo Boo, and Mama June: From Not to Hot.

Due to her TV fame, she has been able to amass a net worth of $400,000, according to Celebrity Net Worth.

This estimate comes from her TV roles as well as other business ventures. Celebrity Net Worth notes that she's paid $50,000 per episode.

Outside of TLC, Alana has been seen competing on Dancing with the Stars: Juniors and even dabbled in music in 2015 with the song Movin' Up.

Who has custody of Honey Boo Boo?

During Alana's TLC days, fans were also introduced to her parents, mom June "Mama June" Shannon and dad Mike "Sugar Bear" Thompson.

While Alana's parents might be in the spotlight, they're not the ones who have custody of her.

In 2019, she was placed in the care of her half-sister Lauryn "Pumpkin" Efird amid her mother's struggles with addiction and was then granted full custody in June 2022.

“Alana made that choice to stay in that environment,” June said in a 2022 interview with PageSix.

She added: "People don’t understand the custody thing. It’s not like somebody ‘lost custody.’ In the state of Georgia, you do temporary guardianship every year and [Alana] had been with [Lauryn] in my addiction.

"At the age of 12, you’re able to make your choice.”

June went on to note that she has "good" relationships with all of her daughters, including Jessica Shannon and Anna Cardwell, and while she might not live with Alana the pair communicate frequently.

"I do see her, I do talk to her. We do communicate," she told the outlet.

"So it wasn’t like any difference. The only difference is she’s not sitting right here beside me in my home every day.”

Along with being awarded custody, the courts also ordered June to pay Lauryn $800 a month in child support until she turns 18 in August 2023, PageSix notes.

He previously attended a Nashville technical school before the Covid-19 pandemic began but now resides in Georgia near Alana.

At first, fans loved seeing Alana happy amid all the drama with her mom, but their opinions of her relationship quickly changed after they discovered the age difference between them.

Alana began dating Dralin when she was 16 and he was 21.

Despite the age gap, Alana revealed during an interview with Entertainment Tonight that her family supports her relationship.

"In our relationship, the two most things that everybody is always talking about is our age gap and definitely because he's Black and I’m white and we're an interracial couple," Alana said.

"I don’t care because, like, at the end of the day, my sister approves, his mom approves, and we're happy, so what fans got to say or what haters got to say I don’t really care."

Pumpkin confirmed her sister's claims, revealing that she thinks her sister is emotionally mature enough to date an older man.

"She’s not getting married, she’s not having a kid, she's doing very good in school," Pumpkin explained.

She went on to add that she thinks Alana and her man are "on the same wavelength."

At this time, it is unclear how Alana and Dralin met.
