Does Paul Reed have a wife? Let’s find out if the Philadelphia 76ers player is happily married or still single.

Paul Reed is an American basketball player.

He currently plays for the Philadelphia 76ers of the NBA. Commonly referred to as “B-Ball Paul out the mud” he previously played college football for the DePaul Blue Demons.

Prior to joining DePaul, he refused several offers from Clemson, Kansas State, Rutgers, and Murray State. He began his professional career in 2020 where he was drafted in the second round with 58th overall selection in the 2020 NBA draft by the Philadelphia 76ers.

Additionally, Paul made his debut on January 4, 2021, playing the final 90 seconds of the game and scoring two points on his sole field goal attempt in a 118–101 win over the Charlotte Hornets.

Most recently, he was named the 2021 NBA G League Most Valuable Player and Rookie of the Year.
