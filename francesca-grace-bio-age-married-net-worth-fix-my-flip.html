Meet HGTV Francesca Grace! Bio, Age, Married, Net Worth, Fix My Flip

One more time, HGTV is lending a helping hand to people in dire need of transforming their struggling project into a profitable one. Yes. That’s Fix My Flip we are talking about. The show premiered on 10 March 2022 with real estate expert Page Turner also putting in her own money as a producer. Starring as one of the three leads in the show is also star entrepreneur Francesca Grace Chiloiro, and professional contractor-builder Mitch Glew.

Now, the rest of the writing is especially about Francesca Grace. All set to showcase her aesthetic on national television and she still managed to stay low-key and that is about to change now.

Francesca Grace On HGTV’s Fix My Flip

HGTV’s Fix My Flip introduces Francesca Grace as an entrepreneur and proud owner of a home staging service, an interior design studio, as well as home decor, called Francesca Grace Home.

So, with this television job, the celebrity interior design and home stager won’t just be helping flippers who are stuck, losing money and in over their heads, she will also be bringing her team some top dollars. At the same time, her skills and service will be acknowledged on that big of a platform. Meaning, she will be having more clients.

A true tastemaker, her fashion-forward aesthetic and chic California style with whimsical prints, bold colors, vintage finds, and maximalist design are certainly going to bring in good reviews to the new six-episode series; it was filmed with the natives of Southern California.

Francesca was officially allowed to announce that they were starting shooting Fix My Flip in May last year. “So excited to be working with such an incredible network and crew” Francesca had said tagging along with her two co-hosts.

Francesca Grace Net Worth

Francesca Grace reportedly held $750K as net worth as of March 2022.

In recent times, other than working as a CEO/founder of Francesca Grace Home, she was a CEO at Thrifted Fox and a production assistant at Sunrise Brands Corporate Office.

From 2013 through 2016, Francesca studied Merchandise Marketing at The Fashion Institute of Design & Merchandising, a private for-profit college with multiple campuses in California.

Briefly, in 2013, Francesca also assisted her boss with pulling outfits for commercials, television, magazine covers, as well as other projects and Styling celebrity clientele at Ali Levine Design. Just like that, she has done several other jobs; fashion stylist, public relations intern, social media manager, but each one for less than a year. And it seems she has been the boss of her own since March 2016.

On LinkedIn, Francesca commends herself saying “With my educational background, as well as my business, styling, and social media experience, I feel I am ready to take on my dreams of working in the merchandise marketing world as a successful and well-communicated spirit.”
