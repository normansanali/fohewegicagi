Harry Souttar Height: How tall is Harry Souttar?

Harry James Souttar is a professional footballer who plays as a defender for the EFL Championship club Stoke City. Souttar was born on 22 October 1998, in Aberdeen, Scotland. However, He plays for the Australian national team rather than his country of birth.

How tall is Harry Souttar?

Harry Souttar stands at an average height of 1.98 meters (6 ft 6 in), Souttar is the second tallest player to have represented the Australian national team.

Souttar started his career with Dundee United where he made three first-team appearances. Souttar’s performances caught the eye of Stoke City who made a move for him in September 2016.

However, the Australian defender could not break into the first team in his first few seasons. He was sent on loan to gain experience at Ross County and Fleetwood Town before breaking into the first team at Stoke in 2020–21
