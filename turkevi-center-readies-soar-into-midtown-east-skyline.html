Turkevi Center Readies to Soar into Midtown East Skyline

Inside, will be some not-so-exciting diplomatic functions such as passport and visa centers, offices and meeting rooms. Below ground will be an auditorium and parking for 20 vehicles (this explains the project's deep foundation). Starting on the 20th floor will be 20 residential units available for privileged members of the Republic of Turkey. The building will also have a prayer room, function room, multi-purpose room and two outdoor terraces. According to the project's structural engineers, DeSimone, completion is now anticipated for 2020, pushed back from 2018.
