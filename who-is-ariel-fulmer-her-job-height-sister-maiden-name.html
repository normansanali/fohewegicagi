Who Is Ariel Fulmer? Her Job, Height, Sister, Maiden Name

Ariel Fulmer is the wife of The Try Guy’s Ned Fulmer. After the recent cheating scandal, fans have been sending words of support to her. This article details related to her job, maiden name, sister, and family members. Keep scrolling down and keep learning as this article proceeds.

Meet Ariel Fulmer, Ned Fulmer Wife

Ariel Fulmer is married to Ned Fulmer.

Towards the end of February 2022, Ariel posted on her IG, “Sometimes life goes up and other times it goes down. Right now I’m just feeling grateful for the ride!” A year before the scandal went public, Ariel captioned a post, “Every happy marriage needs a little time apart! One day left to pre-order our Date Night Cookbook! Link in bio.”

The couple shares two children; Wesley, 4 (born April 2018), and Finley, 1 (born November 2020). Ariel announced getting pregnant with Finley sharing a picture of the sonogram.

However, the lovely family of four is in shambles now. Ned has been accused of cheating on Ariel with his co-worker/production assistant Alexandria Herring. The allegation was made by a Reddit user (@hamilton390) claiming that Ned cheated on his wife. Thus, igniting the rumor that the father of two had an extramarital affair with Alexandria.

The Redditor also claimed that he found Ned kissing another woman at a New York club. The speculation of the affair only got worse when fans noticed that Ned Fulmer wasn’t present in any of The Try Guys’ recent videos and podcasts.

Moreover, Page Six reported that Popular YouTube group The Try Guys has fired Ned Fulmer after he admitted cheating on Ariel. “Ned Fulmer is no longer working with The Try Guys,” a post on their Instagram page read. “As a result of a thorough internal review, we do not see a path forward together. We thank you for your support as we navigate this change,” concluded the statement from remaining members Keith Habersberger, Zach Kornfeld, and Eugene Lee Yang.

Less than an hour later, Ned took to his IG and wrote, “Family should have always been my priority, but I lost focus and had a consensual workplace relationship,” he wrote after being seen kissing Try Guys producer Alex Herring at a New York City bar.

“I’m sorry for any pain that my actions may have caused to the guys and the fans but most of all to Ariel,” he continued. “The only thing that matters right now is my marriage and my children, and that’s where I am going to focus my attention.”

Ariel addressed the scandal on her Instagram. On the post, she wrote, “Thanks to everyone who has reached out to me – it means a lot. Nothing is more important to me and Ned than our family, and all we request right now is that you respect our privacy for the sake of our kids.”

Ariel has also appeared on the Try Guys video, Try Wives segments, Try Moms segments, and the “You Can Sit With Us” Podcast.

When Did Ariel Fulmer And Ned Fulmer Get Married?

The Try Guy‘s Ned Fulmer married his wife Ariel Fulmer in June 2012. On their 9th wedding anniversary, Ariel took to her IG and wrote, “@nedfulmer and I are celebrating 9 years of marriage today! Here’s to 900 more hon. I love you so much.”

How Did Ned Fulmer And Ariel Fulmer Meet?

During the third episode of You Can Sit With Us, The Try Wives Podcast, Ariel Fulmer shared a story of how they met. According to her, they met each other in November 2009 when the two were fresh out of college. The two met at the same party when they were introduced by a common acquaintance after just relocating to Chicago.

“Ned describes the whole situation as, like, he saw me when he first walked into the party, and there was nobody else,” Ariel reveals on the podcast.

Ariel Fulmer Job

Ariel Fulmer is an Interior Designer, Stylist, Podcast Host, and Cookbook Author. In January 2015, Ariel Fulmer founded Fig + Stone Designs, so she is a businesswoman. A California-based designer, curator, and creator have expertise in vintage decor, textiles, furniture, and art. Fig + Stone Designs is an online resource for items that have been hand-picked for clients’ homes.

Additionally, she also provides rental service, styling/DIY, E-design, and full-service interior design.

In 2012, after years of chemistry courses and apprenticeships in art conservation, Ariel realized that sitting behind an easel wasn’t for her. She went on to the corporate world and became a buyer for a large online decor retailer. Fast forward five years and she is designing and making pieces of her own. Ariel uses her training from both the art and business world every day with her hands-on approach to every project.

Before that, she worked at One Kings Lane as Associate Category Manager, of Vintage + Market Finds from 2013 to 2015.

Her LinkedIn reveals that Ariel has also worked as Conservation Assistant at The Newberry. From 2009 to 2013, Ariel took the job of Assistant Registrar and Registrar at The Conservation Center. Moreover, her career started as an inventory assistant at Chris Beetles Gallery in 2005 followed by work as an Undergraduate Student at Carleton College.

What Is Ariel Fulmer Maiden Name?

Ariel Fulmer’s maiden name is Ariel Vandevoorde. She was born to her parents Mark and Kathryn Vandevoorde.

Currently 64 years old, her father is currently working as a freelance business consultant. Mark had previously worked for companies such as GCC Bunkers, AM Agrigen Industries, and Unipec America, Inc. among others.

Ariel’s mother Kathryn, who turned 63 in October 2021, is an owner at Healthcare Reliability Solutions, LLC. She also worked at Memorial Hermann. She studied Pre-Pharmacy at Washington State University and earned her Doctorate of Pharmacy at the University of Southern California.

Who Is Ariel Fulmer Sister?

Ariel Fulmer has a sister named Danielle Vandevoorde. In September 2022, Danielle turned 34 years old. Ariel featured her on National Siblings Day. She wrote, “Happy #nationalsiblingday to my forever ride or die @dvandevoodoo I would Thelma and Louise to First Aid Kit with you any day. Just say the word.”

Talking about her job, Danielle is currently working as Project Manager, Advancement Fundraising at Cedars-Sinai. She attended The American School in London in 2007. She also attended The University of Queensland. Ariel earned a Bachelor of Science from Skidmore College.

Danielle studied Global Supply Chain Management, Logistics, Materials, and Supply Chain Management at Bayes Business School. She graduated in 2018.
