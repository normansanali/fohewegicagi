Who Is Brenda Flint? Get To Know Jacob Clint Wife! Her Age, Job

Country singer Jacob Flint or Jake Flint died hours after his wedding to his new wife Brenda Flint. Jacob’s longtime publicist Clif Doyal confirmed the news of the singer’s death, the news outlet reported.

‘He was not only a client, he was a dear friend and just a super nice guy. As you can see from the outpouring on social media, he was loved by everybody. I think a lot of it was just that he was a people person, and he had an amazing sense of humor. He made everybody laugh, and he made everybody feel welcome,’ Doyal said.

‘He was an ambassador for Oklahoma Red Dirt music.’

So, who is Brenda Flint? How old is she? What does she do as a job? Find out all of it and more here in this article about Jake Flint’s wife Brenda Flint Wilson.

Meet Brenda Flint, Late Country Singer Jacob Flint Wife

Oklahoman country singer Jacob Clint has died at the age of 37. The cause of death has not yet been determined. The singer died just hours after exchanging his wedding vows. His wedding with Brenda Clint took place at a remote homestead between Claremore and Owasso near Tulsa.

It was a rainy day, according to musician Mike Hosty, who performed at their nuptials. “I still have the mud in my old van … but it was just a fun wedding,” Hosty told The Oklahoman. “When any musician asks you to play at their wedding, it’s one of those most important days … and it’s always an honor.”

“We should be going through wedding photos but instead I have to pick out clothes to bury my husband in. People aren’t meant to feel this much pain,” Brenda wrote heartbreakingly. “People aren’t meant to feel this much pain. My heart is gone and I just really need him to come back. I can’t take much more. I need him here.”

One day prior, she shared a video with her late husband seemingly taken at their wedding. “I don’t understand,” she penned.

Back in April 2022, Brenda had also posted on her Facebook, “As most of you know, this handsome fella has recently been forced to take an unfortunate, but medically necessary, hiatus from doing what he loves most, and has had to cancel more shows than he probably ever has in his entire career.”

She added, “But does he let that get him down? Hell no! While most of us would be wallowing in self-pity, he simply switches gears, continuing to make our home a home by clearing and tilling us a beautiful garden, and doing the same for countless others who are unable to do so themselves.”

Brenda concluded the post, “He never stops the grind, never gives up or feels sorry for himself, and does it all with a selfless, loving heart.”

The married couple celebrated their 2 year anniversary (dating) in August 2022. Brenda had posted on her FB, “You babe. You’re my bestest friend. 2 years of love and lots of laughter, a lifetime left to go. Happy anniversary, my love.” They reportedly got engaged in mid-January 2022.

Jacob announced the engagement with a post writing, “Oops. Say hello to the future Mrs. Flint.” 

Does Brenda Flint Have Kids?

Brenda Flint Age

Brenda Flint was born on 15 January 1984. In January 2022, Brenda celebrated her 38th birthday.

Brenda Flint Job

Per Brenda Flint’s socials, she was employed at an Irish pub named J Farley’s. The pub’s FB bio reads, “We are an Americanized Irish Pub, serving traditional pub fare along with some Irish selections in our “Across the Pond” menu category. J Farley’s is one of Claremore’s favorite places for great food and entertainment for the whole family.”

Brenda has been working at the pub at least since 2020.

On the day of Brenda and Jacob’s wedding, the pub’s IG page posted, “Today we celebrated the marriage of Jake and Brenda as a J Farley’s family. Many of us here and many of our customers as well, have got to witness this sweet love story unfold as it started here at J Farley’s and only grew bigger!”

The post concluded, “Brenda and Jake we love you and we wish you both a lifetime of happiness! 💚Congratulations Mr. And Mrs. Jake Flint.”
