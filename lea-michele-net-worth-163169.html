Lea Michele Net Worth

Lea Michele is an American actress and singer who rose to fame for her role as Rachel Berry in the musical comedy TV series Glee. She was born on August 29, 1986 in New York City, NY and earned multiple Golden Globe Award nominations for her performance. In 2015, she was cast as Hester in the FOX horror/comedy series Scream Queens.
