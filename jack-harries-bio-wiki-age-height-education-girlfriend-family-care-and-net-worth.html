    Harries Career

    Little did Jack know that his way of busting pre-exam stress by watching YouTube videos, would one day pave the way for his career. Sans any great expectations, a curious Jack, a media student back then, took to YouTube to share his traveling stories and his insights about how to make the world a better place. Videos that were meant to be shared among his friends and family, made their way to more than thousands of people around the globe and he soon found himself surrounded by appreciation and acceptance. His YouTube channel, named JacksGap, has gained 3.71 million subscribers and has been educating the youth about different climatic conditions and the lives of the poor in different parts of the world. Not just through YouTube, He runs a blog where people can not only delve deeper into the causes they want to stand for but can participate in forums and discussions. He is seen traveling to several parts of the world such as India, Istanbul and Tokyo, together with his travel buddy, humanitarian, and his identical twin brother Finn. Their travel stories will leave you filled with awe and that is just an understatement! This twenty-three-year-old and his untiring efforts toward shaping the world and inspiring the youth to no bounds are a few of his special qualities. His Instagram and Twitter are brimming with the faces of the people he met during his travels and bringing to light their plight and struggle. In this way, he has encouraged his growing fans to take time out of their busy lives to do something small-yet-life-changing for the underprivileged.
