Rema Becomes First Afrobeats Artiste To Achieve This Feat In India (DETAILS)

Afrobeats singing sensation and Jonzing World act, Divine Ikubor, professionally known as Rema has hit a new career milestone in the Nigerian music industry.

Ever since he made his breakthrough onto the music scene, the talented rap artiste has been the center of attraction, churning out back-to-back hits whiles delivering classic verses on songs he’s featured on.

The young Nigerian musician and Mavin Records prodigy who continues to spread the gospel of African music movement across the globe has marked his belt with yet another enviable achievement in India with his monster hit song, ‘Calm Down’.

On Friday, 11th February 2022 the award-winning recording and performing artiste served the music community with ‘Calm Down’. As continues his rapid rise to the top of African music royalty, his song ‘Calm Down’ has sold 60 thousand units in India which is equivalent to 12 million streams.

The achievement makes the tune eligible for a Gold certification from the most reputable recording industry in the country; the Indian Music Industry. Congratulations to the young champ.

MEANWHILE: Renowned Nigerian recording and performing artiste, Oluwatobiloba Daniel Anidugbe, professionally called Kizz Daniel is having a good run with his recent output.

On Friday, October 14, 2022, the award-winning singer, entertainer, and father of two served his listeners and the entire music community with an amazing piece of music captioned, ‘Cough (Odo)’.

The beautifully crafted single, ‘Cough (Odo)’ which has already generated a dance challenge on social media debuted on Apple Music Charts in several countries across the globe in less than 24 hours of its release.
