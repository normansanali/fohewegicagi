Free Match: AJ Styles vs Shinsuke Nakamura IWGP Intercontinental Title Match from Wrestle Kingdom 10

Every Monday, New Japan Pro Wrestling posts a completely free match on both YouTube and New Japan World. This week’s free bout features the “Phenomenal One” AJ Styles taking on Shinsuke Nakamura for the IWGP Intercontinental Championship at Wrestle Kingdom 10.

Not only would this match go down as the final New Japan singles match for both men before signing contracts with WWE, it is an instant classic and one of the best championship matches in either man’s library. For those interested in seeing some of Styles’ best work in Japan, or for WWE fans who have never seen what “big match Nakamura” is capable of on a massive stage, this is a perfect selection to check out.

If you want to see Styles vs. Nakamura or any of the other Wrestle Kingdom 10 bouts featuring English commentary instead of the traditional Japanese announcers, all content is available on for less than $9 per month. Both AJ Styles and Shinsuke Nakamura will participate in the annual WWE Money in the Bank ladder match for the very first time at next month’s pay-per-view of the same name, alongside U.S. Champion Kevin Owens, Sami Zayn, Baron Corbin, and Dolph Ziggler.
