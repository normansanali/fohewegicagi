Erica Hubbard Net Worth

Erica Hubbard is an American actress born on January 2, 1979 in Chicago, IL. She is best known for her roles in the BET series Let's Stay Together, the ABC Family drama Lincoln Heights, the films A Cinderella Story and Akeelah and the Bee. She has been a successful actress for many years and continues to be a popular figure in the entertainment industry.
