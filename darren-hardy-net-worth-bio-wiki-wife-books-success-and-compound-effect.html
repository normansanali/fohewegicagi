Darren Hardy Success Magazine

At the age of 18, Darren began his first business. For 25 + years, he has been a key figure in the success media business. He became publisher of the magazine SUCCESS and Success Media in 2007. Hardy announced on December 2015 that he was leaving SUCCESS magazine to pursue new opportunities as a publisher.

Darren held executive positions in two personal development-focused television networks prior to this position. He was The People’s Network’s (TPN) executive producer and master distributor and chairman of The Success Training Network (TSTN). Darren is best known for his popular book “The Compound Effect,” which has been cited as his best work to date by top critics.

Hardy was awarded the National Speakers Association (NSA) ‘ Master of Influence ‘ designation in honor of his public speaking professionalism.
