The Muddy Truth About Kylie Jenner's Billionaire Status Today

Kylie Jenner is not only known for her looks but also for her growing business empire. The 24-year-old mother of two made a fortune from her first appearance in 2007 on the reality tv show Keeping Up With The Kardashians, but her sources of income do not stop from there. With the help of their millions of followers and business-minded Momager Kris Jenner, there is no surprise that Kylie is next in line with her sister Kim Kardashian on the billionaire's list.

But as the youngest Kardashian-Jenner billionaire in the family, is her short journey to wealth also fall from it? How did she do it, and can the others catch up to her soon? Here's how Kylie Jenner became the youngest billionaire in their clan...

How Did Kylie Jenner Become A Billionaire So Fast?

Kylie Jenner amassed her $1 billion net worth when she was 21 years old. Forbes calls her the youngest self-made billionaire ever when her net worth jumped from an estimated $8,700,000 to a billion in only seven years. From 2014 to 2015, Time Magazine included Kylie Jenner, who was 18 years old and one of the most influential teens in her generation.

In Keeping Up With The Kardashians, Kris Jenner says that Kylie was cut off from any financial assistance when she turned eighteen. However, some fans are skeptical if this is true, knowing that Kris still handled their business affairs as their manager. But it is enough evidence to show that Kylie Jenner did not depend on her family's wealth for her status.

How Much Does Kylie Jenner Make A Day?

Twitter fans believe Kylie Jenner is making up to $450,000 a day, considering all her income sources. However, Business Insider makes a different estimate of her daily income, which they believe could be more than fans think. Her daily income sparked controversy when tweets surfaced about Kylie indirectly asking her fans to pay for her $60,000 worth of medical bills when she easily earns them in more than three hours. But Kylie did not release any statement confirming her daily income, nor did she address her Twitter fans' arguments about her bills.

How Many Businesses Does Kylie Jenner Have?

Although there is no official count on how many businesses she owns, Forbes estimates Kylie has 120 trademarks on different brand names. Kylie Cosmetics which started in 2015, propelled her billionaire status when her make-up line reached $1.2 billion in worth in 2022.

Kylie then ventured into clothes from only selling lipsticks in 2015 when she collaborated with Kendall in their business called Kendall + Kylie. Still, it did not reach the billion-dollar level as her previous business.

Not all of Kylie's businesses have her successful magic touch. Kylie Swim is also an example of Kylie's company that people call a flop. Kylie received customer backlash on her swimwear line when they reviewed its poor quality cloth and sizing. Kylie Cosmetics also had its fair share of issues when 2021 fans now are calling it "not a worth buy" because of its unethical workers' conditions.

Is Kylie Jenner Still A Billionaire In 2023?

With all the ups and downs she has experienced in the last few years, Forbes calculates that Kylie Jenner is not a billionaire anymore—and questions if she ever was. They estimated her present net worth to be $900 million, $100 million less to retain her billionaire status. With a 62 percent decline in online sales of her existing businesses and the slow growth of her new ones, Kylie seems to be struggling to maintain the hype in her cosmetic and clothing businesses alone.

Other sources have reported her net worth to be as low as $750 million, though this still makes her one of the most successful female celebrities in the world.

Forbes says, "Kylie's business quietly fell by more than half in a single year," following their removal of Kylie's name on their billionaire list. Forbes speculates that Kylie or her camp may be hiding or lying about the actual revenue she earns from her different businesses to begin. The fast decline of her income even during the pandemic sparked the attention of Evercore analyst Omar Saad saying, "It seems unlikely that much revenue could have evaporated overnight."

However, although Forbes has kicked Kylie Jenner out of the billionaire table, her current nine-digit net worth is still enough to sustain her and her family's lifestyle.
