Wiki/Biography

Ramatu Ujiri was born as ‘Ramatu Barry’ in 1986 (age 34 years; as in 2020) in Freetown, Sierra Leone, West Africa. She moved to the United States to attend Eleanor Roosevelt High School in Maryland. She is currently residing in Toronto, Ontario with her family.

She is currently working as a model. Before modeling, Ramatu Ujiri used to work at Outer Skinz Image Group, a makeup and wardrobe styling company for celebrities.
