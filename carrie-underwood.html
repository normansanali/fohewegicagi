Carrie Underwood News - Us Weekly

Name:Carrie Underwood

Hometown:Checotah, Oklahoma

Birthday:March 10, 1983

About

After winning season 4 of American Idol in 2005, Carrie Underwood instantly shot to fame. Her 2005 debut album, Some Hearts, has since become the best-selling solo female debut in country history and earned her three Grammy Awards, including Best New Artist.

Subsequent albums Carnival Ride (2007), Play On (2009) and Blown Away (2012) found similar commercial and critical success, solidifying her as one of the top female artists of the 2000s. She has released nine studio albums in total and has earned eight Grammy Awards through the years. Her fitness clothing line, CALIA by Carrie, was launched in 2015. Underwood’s fitness and lifestyle book, Find Your Path, became a New York Times bestseller upon its release in 2020.

Underwood began dating Canadian hockey player Mike Fisher in late 2008, and he popped the question in December 2009. The pair said “I do” in a luxe country ceremony in Georgia in July 2010. They welcomed sons Isaiah and Jacob in February 2015 and January 2019, respectively. Before her second son was born, Underwood shared that she suffered three miscarriages in two years.
