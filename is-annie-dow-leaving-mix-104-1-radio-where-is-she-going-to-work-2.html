Is Annie Dow Leaving Mix 104.1 Radio, Where Is She Going To Work?

Annie Dow is a young US-based journalist known for her show on Mix 104.1. According to her LinkedIn profile, she was a co-host on the Morning Show and a traffic reporter at Entercom.

The talented reporter has been working at Entercom since 2017, located in Boston, MA. The news of the journalist leaving Mix 104.1 Radio is trending on the internet. Read the full article to find out more.

Annie Dow’s Leaving Mix 104.1 Radio

Annie Dow has confirmed leaving Mix 104.1 Radio from her official Twitter account. She has retweeted the post by her co-worker, Kasor Tager, from Mix 104.1 Morning Show.

They have been working together as a team for a long time. The tweet was made on June 27, and Annie has confirmed this tweet. She has shared this tweet on her Twitter feed.

However, the official page of Mix 104.1 Radio has also made a post regarding reporter Dow leaving the show. We can see the post shared by Mix 104.1 on Facebook today.

The journalist has worked with Mix 104.1 for more than four years. She started to work there as a reporter and host in December 2017 and was part of the company till June 2022. She was part of the Karson & Kennedy show there.

The work of the young lady as the reporter and co-host at Mix 104.1 was appreciated by many people. Her work was recognized and praised by the public.

Her fans have expressed how the news of Annie leaving the show has made them sad and heartbroken. They used to love the positivity and love she used to bring to the show.

Where Is Annie Dow Going To Work?

Annie Dow has not decided to take a break from her job. The reporter wants to take some time for herself and is figuring out what is best for her.

The brilliant journalist has not revealed her exact plan after leaving the show. All her fans and well-wishers believe that she will show up again with something amazing.
