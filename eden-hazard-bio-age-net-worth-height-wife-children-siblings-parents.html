Eden Hazard is a famous Belgian professional footballer who plays as a winger or attacking midfielder for La Liga club Real Madrid.

Eden Hazard was the captain of the Belgium national team for many years but retired from National team duties in 2022 after their exit from the 2022 World Cup in Qatar.

Eden Hazard played 126 times for the Belgium national team and scored 33 goals.
