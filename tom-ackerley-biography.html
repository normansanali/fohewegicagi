Tom Ackerley Bio (Wiki)

Who is Tom Ackerley?

Tom Ackerley is a British producer and assistant director. He is popular for movies like The Last Days on Mars (2013) and The Brothers Grimsby (2016).

Furthermore, he came to the limelight after his marriage to an actress, Margot Robbie. They two also have their own film company, Luckychap Entertainment.

Tom Ackerley: Age, Parent, Ethnicity

The actor was born on 1st January 1990 in Surrey, England. He grew up in Guildford, Surrey. As of 2023, he is 32 years old.

His parents’ names are unknown yet. His father is an established Real Estate Agent in the surrey area and his mother owns a property management company.

He has one older brother, Ben Ackerley. He also has a sister but her sister’s name is unknown. Ackerly always aimed to have a career in the entertainment industry from a young age.

Tom belongs to British nationality and is of Caucasian ethnicity.

In which school did Tom Ackerley attend?

Talking about his education, he enrolled at Godalming College with a Bachelor of Arts degree in Film Studies.

Tom Ackerley: Career, Professional life

Tom Ackerley’s firstly worked as a floor runner on the period drama ‘The Hour’ in 2011. That year, he was involved in his first film, ‘War Horse’, as a location runner. In the ensuing two years, he would also serve as a crowd runner and set production assistant before landing his first job as an assistant director in Sky Arts 1 TV series ‘Playhouse Presents.

Tom started his screen debut in the year 2013 with the biographical sports action drama movie Rush alongside Chris Hemsworth, Daniel Bruhl, Olivia Wilde, and Natalie Dormer as well. Additionally, the movie was able to collect $98 million in the box office with a budget of $54 million.

After a year, he also made his appearance in the 2014 English comedy film Pride, featuring Dominic West and George MacKay. Tom made his breakthrough in the 2015 movie Macbeth. His first film as an assistant director was the 2013 science fiction horror ‘The Last Days on Mars’. In the year 2017, he appeared in a commercial promoting Nissan electric vehicles.

Ackerley then worked as an assistant director and producer. He has worked for Macbeth, Pride, and The Two Faces of January.  More recently, he’s gained production credits on two of Margot’s projects: the ice-skating biopic I, Tonya, in which his wife plays disgraced figure skater Tonya Harding, and Terminal, a thriller.

Ackerley has his wife have their own film company, Luckychap Entertainment in 2014, with Tom’s close friend, Josey McNamara, and Margot’s best friend, Sophia Kerr, and many more. Since then, they’ve produced seven projects including I, Tonya, Terminal, Dreamland, and the upcoming Birds of Prey and Barbie.

As one of the producers of I, Tonya, Ackerley was nominated for Gotham Independent Film Award for Best Feature Film and the Producers Guild of America Award for Best Theatrical Motion Picture.

Tom Ackerley: Salary, Net Worth

This director’s estimated net worth is around $4 million as of 2023.

He has worked in several successful movies like Rush, Pride, and Macbeth. Rush’s production cost was $52 million and it grossed a box office collection of $98 million all over the world. His main sources of income are his acting career and his own business.

Furthermore, his wife Robbie has a net worth of $22 million.

In early 2016, the couple bought the house for $2.73 million nestled in Hancock Park, a residential neighborhood in Los Angeles, California. The house covers a 3,300-square-foot area with a five-bedroom and a 5.5-bathroom.
