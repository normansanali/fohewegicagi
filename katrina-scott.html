Professional Life

Later, after her graduation, she began working at fitness clubs and has several certifications as a ‘master trainer’.

She decided to start a YouTube channel, “Tone It Up “for creating content based on fitness and a healthy lifestyle. Scott joined YouTube on September 6, 2008. Her YouTube channel has amassed 72 million views altogether.
