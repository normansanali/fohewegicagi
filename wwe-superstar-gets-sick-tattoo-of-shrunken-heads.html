WWE Superstar Gets Sick Tattoo Of Shrunken Heads

WWE’s roster of Superstars looks much different than in previous generations. It is hard to find a Superstar without ink. Baron Corbin has plenty of tattoos and the King just added another one.

Corbin is a big fan of obscure collectibles. He already owns shrunken head named Nigel. Now he has a few tattooed on him. The 2019 King Of The Ring was happy to show off the outline of a new tattoo he got of some shrunken heads.

Well the side of the knee sucks but these shrunken heads will be worth it

This tattoo is going to look really cool once it’s finished. It appears that Corbin is getting to work on this new piece. Let’s see how many sessions it takes or if he was able to get it all done in one sitting.
